    • RICE PUDDING


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Tablespoon ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Cup Raisins
  • 1 LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk Can
  • 1 CARNATION® CLAVEL® Evaporated Milk Can
  • 1 Cup Rice soaked in hot water for 10 minutes and drained
  • 4 1/2 Cups Water
  • 1 Piece Cinnamon in raja
    • PREPARATION:


    Heat the water with cinnamon and when it boils, add the rice; cook for 15 minutes or until the rice is tender.

    Pour LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk, CARNATION® CLAVEL® Evaporated Milk and raisins; mix and continue cooking at low heat for 20 to 25 minutes until lightly thickened.

    Serve warm or cold and sprinkle with ground cinnamon.





    BROWNIES


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 100 g. of butter
  • 200 g. of dark chocolate 70% minimum (the brand you like the most)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100 g of normal or icing sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon (dessert type) of baking soda.
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence
  • 80 g of wheat flour
  • Of 50 g. to 100 g. of nuts (depends on what you like)

    • PREPARATION:


    The first thing is to prepare the container where we are going to cook them. I used a tray of 20 x 20cm glass and baking paper.

    The measure of the tray gives for 9 generous brownies, but everything depends on how we want it to be thick. If you want them smaller, use a larger tray.

    We cover the tray and spread it with butter. Sprinkle the oven paper with chocolate powder. I have not used flour because the last time I prepared it, there were small specks of white flour in the base.

    With this trick we solve this problem and on top we give more chocolate flavor to this cake.

    We put in a bowl the grated chocolate or in small pieces with butter and put it in the microwave for 4 minutes at medium temperature. We take out and mix the mixture well with a stick until it is a cream similar to chocolate cream.

    Beat the eggs with the sugar in a large bowl until there is a foamy cream. Add the tablespoon of vanilla and baking soda. Pour the chocolate cream in the previous bowl and mix well with the rods.

    We sieve the flour with a sieve and add to the previous mixture. We stir very well until we obtain a homogeneous cream paste.

    We cut the nuts into pieces and passed them through a little chocolate powder. We put them in the cream and with a fork we cover them well with the chocolate.

    We bake the brownie in the preheated oven at 180º C in the middle part for 35-40 minutes with the hot oven up and down (without air).

    As I mentioned in some other recipe, it all depends on the oven, always look with a fork that comes out dry when pricked.

    When it has been 20 minutes in the oven, cover with a little aluminum foil so that the surface does not burn and the crust is perfect and crunchy.

    In my oven, the preparation time is exactly 40 minutes, but in my mother's 30-35 minutes it arrives. You do not have to go through time because you will get some dry cakes that nobody can eat them.

    We let it cool on the tray and when it is cold we cut it into several portions. We separate them from the oven paper and present them on a plate with a bit of vanilla or chocolate ice cream, or with hot chocolate. For the most demanding chocolate palates.





    CAPIROTADA


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 1/4 cup of piloncillo
  • 1 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/4 cup of raisins
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 3/4 cup Cotija cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup of raisins
  • 16 slices of bread roll 1/3 inch at least two days before
  • 1/4 cup of peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons of butter in cubes
    • PREPARATION:


    Preheat the oven to 350oF (180oC).

    In a medium saucepan heat the piloncillo, cinnamon, cloves and water at medium temperature so that the piloncillo melts to form a syrup.
    Mix the butter - previously melted - with the oil and varnish with this mixture the slices of bread. Place the slices of bread in a tray and put them in the oven for 8 minutes. Then turn the slices of bread so that they have a golden brown and leave them approx. 5 more minutes until they are a light golden color.

    Now follows the process of assembling the capirotada: Start placing the slices of bread on the base of a baking dish and cover each of these with the brown sugar syrup. (Make sure each slice is well soaked in this syrup). If you prefer soak the bread in the syrup first and then put it in the mold.

    Cover this first layer of bread with cheese, raisins, peanuts and any other fruit or nuts that you have decided to add.

    Place another layer of bread and continue with the same process as in the previous step.

    When you have finished with all the layers, empty the rest of the syrup; cover them with cheese, raisins and peanuts, and place the butter cubes on top. Cover the mold with aluminum foil and bake for about 45 minutes. until all layers of bread are moistened and a golden layer has formed.




    CHOCO-FLAN


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cake box Chocolate Fudge Betty Crocker ™ SuperMoist®
  • 360 ml of caramel syrup
  • 1 cup of water
  • ½ cup of vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • Flan
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 4 eggs
    • PREPARATION:


    Heat the oven to 175 ° C. Grease a 12-cup screw mold with butter. Pour ¼ cup of caramel syrup into the bottom of the pan. Refrigerate the rest of the candy to serve on the cake.

    In a large bowl, beat the cake mix, water, oil and 3 eggs with an electric mixer at medium speed for 2 minutes, scraping the bowl occasionally. Empty the mixture over the caramel in the mold.

    Blend the flan ingredients for 40 seconds at high speed. Pour slowly over the cake mixture in the mold. (The mixes of the cake and the flan will be combined, but will be separated after having baked them.)

    Cover the mold with aluminum foil. Place it on a large baking tray and add 2 cm of hot water to the tray.

    Bake for an hour until when you insert a toothpick into the cake it comes out clean. Remove the mold from the baking tray and place it on a cooling rack, remove the foil. Allow to cool completely, approximately one hour.

    Place a service plate of head on the screw mold, and turn both to unmold the cake. Remove the mold. Cut the chocoflan into slices and serve with the rest of the caramel syrup. Keep it in the refrigerator.




    FLANES


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 5 pieces of whole egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 pinch of optional cinnamon powder
  • 1 can of half cream (optional)
  • 1 piece of cream cheese 190 g (optional)
    • PREPARATION:


    Preheat oven to 175 ° C

    Sprinkle the sugar at the bottom of the mold where you are going to cook it and burn it on the stove burner, let it cool.

    Blend all the ingredients until they are incorporated very well, add to the mold that the sugar will already be cold and hard.

    Put it in the oven in a bain-marie (inside another mold with a little water) take care that no water enters the pudding. If you do not want it to brown, cover it with aluminum foil.

    Leave it in the oven for 45 minutes at approximately 170 ° and until you chop it with a knife and it comes out clean. If at that moment you want to brown it remove the foil and let it brown for 10 minutes. Take it out and let cool. Refrigerate and unmold it, enjoy it cold.

    STRAWBERRY JAM


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 kilo of clean strawberries
  • 4 cups of white sugar
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice rea
    • PREPARATION:


    In a large bowl, mash the strawberries little by little until you have 4 cups of crushed fruit. In a cast iron saucepan, mix the strawberries, sugar and lemon juice. Cook over low heat, continuing to move, until the sugar has dissolved. Raise the heat to high and let the mixture boil, continuing to move, until it reaches a temperature of 105 ° C (220 ° F).

    Pour into sterile and hot jars, leaving about 1 cm of space between the jam and the neck of the jar. Close well. Cool in a water bath if you are going to consume immediately, otherwise, just refrigerate.




    MUFFINS


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup of Milk (240 milliliters)
  • 1 cup of flour (140 grams)
  • 2 dessert spoons of Baking powder.
  • ½ teaspoon of salt.
  • ½ cup of Sugar (100 grams)
  • 2 Eggs.
  • 1 tablespoon of Butter.
    • PREPARATION:


    Making muffins at home is very simple, you just need to gather the right ingredients and the right utensils, like a big bowl, cupcake molds, electric or manual rods and a colander. That said, we started beating the butter at room temperature with the sugar.

    Then, to get the easy-to-make homemade muffins to be fluffy, we separated the white from the yolks of the eggs. We introduce the yolks to the previous mixture and continue beating.

    Then pour the milk and continue mixing to integrate all the ingredients. We sieve the flour with the baking powder and salt, add this mixture and incorporate it with a spatula, making enveloping movements. Besides, we preheat the oven to 200 ºC.

    To enhance the flavor of homemade muffins, you can add vanilla essence, lemon juice or cinnamon powder. Once the dough is ready, we take 3/4 of the molds and bake for 20-30 minutes at 180 ºC with heat up and down. Depending on the furnace it may take less time to be done, so it is important to monitor them.

    We puncture one with a toothpick and if it comes out completely clean it will mean that the homemade muffins are already there and we can remove them from the oven. Let them cool on a rack before serving. Once cold, we can decorate them with icing sugar, chocolate sauce, caramel, dulce de leche, or what we like the most! As you can see, these are easy-to-make, quick and delicious muffins.

    CARLOTA DESSERT


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 6 pieces of lemon
  • 1 can of evaporated milk (360 ml)
  • 1 can of condensed milk (397 g)
  • 2 packages of cookie Mary (170 g)
    • PREPARATION:


    Squeeze the lemons and reserve the juice.
