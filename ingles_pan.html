    • SHELLS


    INGREDIENTS:

  • FOR THE MASS OF THE BISCAKE
  • 14 gr of fresh yeast
  • 130 ml of whole milk
  • 50 gr of sugar
  • 415 gr of wheat flour
  • 11 gr salt
  • 4 egg
  • 116 g of butter

  • FOR THE PASTA OF THE SHELLS
  • 150 gr of butter
  • 1 vanilla pod cut in half and scraped
  • 225 gr of wheat flour
  • 150 gr of sugar glass


    • FOR THE MASS OF THE BISCAKE

    In the bowl of the blender, add the yeast, milk, sugar, and 55 grams of flour. Mix with a balloon whisk until a paste forms. Change the balloon of the blender by the hook and at low speed let the dough ferment for 15 minutes. Add the remaining flour, salt and eggs and mix at low speed for seven minutes. Add the cold butter and mix at medium speed until the dough is smooth and it takes off from the sides. In a large stainless steel container sprinkled with oil, let the dough rest and ferment in the chamber overnight. The next day, remove the dough on a lightly floured surface and divide it into 50 gram pieces.


    FOR THE PASTA DE LAS CONCHAS

    With the shovel, work the butter and vanilla in the blender until you obtain a smooth mixture. Sift the flour and sugar and integrate them into the butter. Continue beating until you have a soft and flexible paste. Divide the dough into balls of 25 grams. Crush each portion with the palm of your hand until they are seven to eight centimeters in size. To form the shells Bole the dough, place six per tray covered in silicone paper and cover with plastic. Ferment between 20 to 30 minutes or until they double in size. Lightly cover the shells with melted vegetable shortening with a brush. Cover each shell with the flattened shell pasta. Trace the surface of the dough with the shell cutter. Bake at 175ºC for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.




    HORNS


    INGREDIENTS:

  • ½ kilo of wheat flour
  • 10 grams of baking powder
  • 10 grams of sugar
  • 25 grams of milk powder
  • 100 ml of water
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 300 grams of butter or margarine

    • PREPARATION:

    MIX in a bowl, wheat flour, yeast, sugar, milk powder, water, eggs and salt. When you begin to integrate all the ingredients, knead for 10 to 15 minutes or until that the dough no longer sticks on the hands and becomes elastic.

    FORM a square with the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes. Flatten the butter or margarine and form a square (maximum 1 cm thick). Stretch the dough to a square wider than butter.

    Place the butter on the dough, cover the butter with the dough and start to stretch along.
    Fold as if it were a triptych, stretch again and fold 3 more times. Let rest for 30 more minutes STRET the dough again and cut it lengthwise; you must obtain 10 cm wide strips. Make diagonal cuts to obtain triangles. Stretch the dough gently and roll the triangles from the widest part to the narrowest, to get the shape of the little horn.

    Leave them like this to obtain croissants or lower the ends for the little horns. VARNISH with a beaten egg with a tablespoon of milk and bake at 180ºC for 10 to 15 minutes until golden brown.





    DONAS


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 500 gr. Flour 0000
  • 15 gr. Dry yeast or 30 gr. Fresh yeast
  • 100 ml. Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100 gr. Sugar
  • 100 gr. Butter / butter
  • 1 tsp. Salt
    • PREPARATION:


    Add to the warm milk, the yeast, two tablespoons of flour and a spoonful of sugar. We take out all of the same ingredients. Stir it and leave a few minutes until the yeast is activated.

    In a bowl place the flour and add the rest of the sugar in the center, and the yeast activated with the warm milk, sprinkle the salt at the ends so that it does not mix directly with the yeast.
    Add the eggs and integrate all the ingredients.

    Knead for a few minutes, then add the butter, knead and hit the dough on the counter until the butter is completely integrated and the dough returns to take shape (do not add flour).
    Bollar and let rise until doubling its volume, paint with oil the dough so that it does not dry out and cover with a repasador or with paper film. Leave in a warm place.

    Once the volume of the dough is doubled, gasify and stretch with a stick leaving a height of 1 cm. Approximately.
    With a cutter for donuts, or with a glass and another round cutter smaller make the hole in the center.
    Form the donuts and place them in a tray with butter paper separated from each other, let rise again until you double the volume.

    Fry the donuts on both sides in abundant oil at a temperature of 160 degrees. Decorate to taste, it can be with sugar, chocolate, colored granas, icing sugar icing or you can also fill it with dulce de leche or custard.




    BOX BREAD


    INGREDIENTS:

  • 200 grams of flour + the necessary to knead
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of yeast
  • 75 ml of whole milk, at room temperature
    • PREPARATION:


    1. PLACE flour, salt and sugar in a bowl. Add the oil, the yeast, the milk and the egg.
    2. MIX with a spatula and when the ingredients are well integrated, knead for 6 minutes on a clean surface. Let it rest in a warm place for an hour or until it doubles its volume.
    3. PLACE the dough to remove the air that has accumulated, stretch and fold the dough inwards and then in the width. Place in a rectangular mold and let stand for half an hour.
    4. BAKE at 180 ° C for 25 minutes or until bread is golden brown.
    5. LEAVE to cool completely and portion or reserve. Keep in a plastic bag so it does not dry out.
